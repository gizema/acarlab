Granular Materials


    Granular materials are collection of macroscopic particles which show complex, multi-phase behavior. Locomotion on granular surfaces is of interest since energy expenditures of locomotion appendages are often significantly higher compared to the packed, flat surfaces. Loss of wheel traction on granular surfaces can result in immobility of vehicles.
